StudentTracker

Welcome to my StudentTracker project!

The StudentTracker is a website that lets you log in as a teacher to keep track of your music students. Students are listed by instrument and skill level, plus it includes a short bio. Notes can be entered for past lessons as well as goals for future sessions. This project was built using bootstrap, mongo, and node. Click on the button below to get started. If you are a demo user, use the username "demo_user" and the password "demo-pwd".
